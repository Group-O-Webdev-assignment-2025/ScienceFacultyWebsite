Academic Programs

Shape Your Future with Quality Science Education

Undergraduate Programs

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

Duration: 3 Years | Credits: 120

Comprehensive program covering software development, algorithms, data structures, and computer systems. Prepare for careers in software engineering, web development, and systems analysis.

Core Courses: Programming, Algorithms, Database Systems, Web Development

Career Paths: Software Developer, Systems Analyst, Web Developer

Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

Duration: 3 Years | Credits: 120

Focus on networking, system administration, cybersecurity, and IT infrastructure management. Ideal for careers in network administration, IT support, and system security.

Core Courses: Networking, System Administration, Cybersecurity, Database Management

Career Paths: Network Administrator, IT Manager, Security Analyst

Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering

Duration: 3 Years | Credits: 120

Specialize in software design, development methodologies, quality assurance, and project management. Perfect for aspiring software architects and project managers.

Core Courses: Software Design, Agile Development, Testing, Project Management

Career Paths: Software Engineer, Project Manager, QA Engineer

Bachelor of Science in Data Science

Duration: 3 Years | Credits: 120

Master data analysis, machine learning, statistical modeling, and big data technologies. Prepare for roles in data analysis, business intelligence, and AI development.

Core Courses: Statistics, Machine Learning, Data Visualization, Big Data

Career Paths: Data Analyst, Machine Learning Engineer, BI Specialist

Bachelor of Science in Cybersecurity

Duration: 3 Years | Credits: 120

Learn to protect systems and networks from cyber threats, digital forensics, and security protocols. Essential for careers in information security and digital protection.

Core Courses: Network Security, Cryptography, Digital Forensics, Ethical Hacking

Career Paths: Security Analyst, Penetration Tester, Security Architect

Admission Requirements

Academic Qualifications

Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) with at least 2 principal passes in relevant subjects, or equivalent qualifications.

Application Process

Submit completed application form, academic transcripts, and supporting documents through our online portal or admissions office.

Application Deadlines

Applications are accepted throughout the year for January, May, and September intakes. Early application is encouraged.
